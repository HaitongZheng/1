CUT

Mobile app designed for hair fashion lover living in NYC.

2017-2018       UI/UX, Coding

CUT is designed for NYC hair fashion lovers to easily access to the latest hair fashion information on their phones. It provides service such as popular hair style feed, hair salon search and review and professional hair stylist consulting. In a city such as New York, fashion lovers need to be equipped from head to toe. CUT gives such an opportunity for this group of people to focus just on the hair fashion.

Visually, incorporated with artist Marcus Oakley's illustrations, CUT's lively graphics and minimal color palette provides users an fun and easy experience.
