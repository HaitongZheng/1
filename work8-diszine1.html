DIS ZINE #1

Zine on youth culture, art and and social issues.

2018       Book

Dis zine is a series of three zines discussing popular culture, the youth, art and and social issues. The major bodies of Dis zine #1 focus on social issues.

Issue #1's three sections are sequenced in the level of happiness.

Issue #1 is derived from an object from daily life: strawberry flavour gum, thus the color scheme and the topic.
Gum's flavour always fades, just like happiness.
