DIS ZINE #3

Zine on youth culture, art and and social issues.

2018       Editorial

Dis zine is a series of three zines discussing popular culture, the youth, art and and social issues. Dis zine issue #3 focuses on social issues through the lenses of art, culture, technology and politics.

Chapter openings with "EXIT" spelt foreward and backward.

Issue #3 is the only b&w issue in the series. It derives from the word "EXIT", reflecting the coexistence and conflict of reality well as fantasy in today's society.

Only spreads in the middle are in full-color, with screenshots of violent scenes from video games and security cameras posited side by side.
