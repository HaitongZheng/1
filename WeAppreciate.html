"We Appreciate Power" is a song by Canadian musician Grimes, released in 2018. The artist herself stated that by simply listening to the song it will reduce the risk of ending up on any future AI overlord's hit list when it reigns supreme. Lyrically, the song touches on transhumanist ideas such as the future's betterment of the human race,
the possibilities of merging consciousness with machines through mind uploading, and the idea that reality may
be simulated.
